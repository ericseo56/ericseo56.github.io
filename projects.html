DEX

Best Hardware Winner at Cornell Makeathon 2026

a hybrid between an exoskeleton and haptic gloves, DEX was a peripheral gauntlet that was connected to a computer through serial/uart to act as a controller. made with 3 other people, shoutouts to andre, orion, and mic. you can check out live updates to the software by andre at the github link.
